## Domande tecniche e supporto

Per problemi con build, Betaflight, ELRS, VTX, radio, batterie o normativa, il
posto migliore è il forum:

[Vai al forum FPV Italia](https://forum.fpvitalia.com)

Quando apri una discussione, includi modello del drone, componenti principali,
versione firmware, foto dei cablaggi e screenshot degli errori. Aiuta molto chi
ti risponde.

## Collabs and Sponsors

For product sponsorships, review samples, affiliate proposals, advertising,
partnerships or brand collaborations, please contact us by email.

FPV Italia is an Italian FPV drone community and editorial project. We are open to
working with manufacturers, shops and brands when the proposal is relevant to FPV
pilots in Italy and can be presented transparently to our readers.

Please include:

- your company name and official website;
- the product, service or campaign you want to propose;
- whether you are offering a review sample, sponsorship, affiliate collaboration
  or paid placement;
- technical documentation, product pages and shipping availability for Italy/EU;
- any disclosure requirements or campaign deadlines.

We only consider collaborations that can be clearly disclosed and that are useful
for the Italian FPV community. Sending a product does not guarantee a positive
review or publication.

## Privacy e contenuti

Per richieste relative a contenuti pubblicati, link, immagini o informazioni da
correggere, usa GitHub o il forum indicando la pagina specifica e il motivo della
richiesta.
